Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

If you’re on Universal Credit or have a HC3 certificate – next steps

As you’ve told us you are on Universal Credit or have a HC3 (limited help with health costs) certificate, you now have 2 choices. You can pay your Penalty Charge Notice or you can challenge it.

How to pay

To pay your Penalty Charge Notice click the ‘Pay now’ button. You will need a debit or credit card.

How to challenge

To challenge your Penalty Charge Notice you will need to phone or email us.

When you contact us you need to include your reference number. This is in the top left-hand corner of your Penalty Charge Notice letter:

For prescriptions

  • Call us on 0300 330 9291 (this call will be charged at a local rate)
  • Email us at nhsbsa.pecs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

For dental treatment

  • Call us on 0300 330 1293 (this call will be charged at a local rate)
  • Email us at nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
