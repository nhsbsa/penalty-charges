Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

You didn’t have a valid medical exemption certificate at the time, but you can still apply

Although you didn’t have a valid medical exemption certificate when you claimed a free prescription, you still have the chance to apply.

What to do next

You have the following options:

  • Pay the original £8.60 prescription charge, and apply for a medical exemption certificate. If your application is successful you won't have to pay your Penalty Charge.
  • Pay the total amount of £68.60.

If you were not entitled to free prescriptions but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
