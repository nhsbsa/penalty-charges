Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

We need proof that you were entitled to claim free or reduced cost dental treatment between 1 November and 30 November 2017

Type of proof we need to see

We need one example of proof from the following list. Your proof must have been valid at the time you claimed free or reduced cost dental treatment.

Select the reason you were entitled to help with your health costs to see what you need to send:

Award letter

Income-based Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA)

A clear image of every page of your JSA award letter. The image needs to show that your JSA was income-based.

Income Support or income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

A clear image of every page of your Income Support or ESA award letter.

If you’re sending an image of your ESA award letter it needs to show that you receive income-related ESA.

Pension Credit Guarantee Credit

A clear image of your or your partner's Pension Credit Guarantee Credit award letter.

Universal Credit

A clear image of every page of your Universal Credit letter or statement.

Your Universal Credit exemption must have been valid at the time you claimed free dental treatment.

You are entitled to claim free dental treatment if your earnings during your most recent assessment period were:

  • £435 or less
  • £935 or less if your Universal Credit includes a payment for a child or you have limited capability for work or work-related activity

If you claim Universal Credit as a couple, the earnings limit applies to the joint income of you and your partner.


NHS certificate

Maternity exemption

A clear image of either:

    • a MatB1 form issued by a GP or registered midwife
    • a Notification of Birth form issued by a registered midwife
    • a birth or stillbirth certificate
    • your maternity exemption certificate - this will either be a paper certificate you were sent by post or a digital certificate you were sent by email
HC2 certificate

A clear image of your HC2 certificate. The image needs to show the certificate number, valid from, valid until, and for fields.

HC3 certificate

A clear image of your HC3 certificate. The image needs to show the certificate number, valid from, valid until, and for fields.

NHS tax credit exemption

A clear image of your NHS tax credit exemption certificate.

How to send your proof

  1. 1

    Take a photo, scan or screenshot of your proof.

    If you’re taking a photo, use a mobile phone or digital camera.

  2. 2

    Check the image is clear and you can read the information.

  3. 3

    Send the image as an email attachment to: nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

    You must include your reference number 11193492836

    We need to be able to confirm your identity, so your email must also include your:

    • first name and surname (family name)
    • date of birth
    • address including postcode
  4. 4

    Once we receive your email, we will check your proof and contact you within 2 working days.

Or you can send your proof by post

Send a photocopy of your proof to us and include your:

  • reference number 11193492836
  • first name and surname (family name)
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

Send the copy of your proof to:

Once we receive your proof by post, we will check it and contact you within 5 working days.
