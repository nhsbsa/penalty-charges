Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

We cannot find your Income Support or income-related Employment and Support Allowance details

Your exemption certificate was not valid at the time you claimed. This may have been because your certificate had expired or you hadn’t got the certificate in time.

What to do next

Choose one of the following options:

  • Send us proof that you were receiving Income Support or income-related Employment and Support Allowance. If your proof is valid you won’t have to pay your Penalty Charge.
  • Pay the original £8.60 prescription charge, and apply for a maternity exemption certificate. If your application is succesful you won’t have to pay anything.
  • Pay the total amount of £68.60.

If you were not entitled to free prescriptions but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you ask for a review.
