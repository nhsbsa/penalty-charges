Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

We found an HC3 certificate with a higher contribution amount – you still owe £90

A change in circumstances may have meant you got a new HC3 certificate with a higher contribution amount.

You do not have to pay an extra charge, but now owe the revised amount of £90.

What to do next

Choose one of the following

  • Pay the outstanding amount of £90.
  • If you were pregnant or getting benefits at the time you claimed, you can continue your challenge. You may have been entitled to free dental treatment.

If you were not entitled to free dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.

How to avoid a future Penalty Charge Notice

We match exemptions automatically using a combination of your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • NHS number

Make sure these are correct at your GP surgery, dental practice, and any benefits organisations.

Contact us to check the details are correct on your NHS exemption

For medical and maternity exemption certificates, and prescription prepayment certificates

  • Call us on 0300 330 1341

For NHS tax credit exemption certificates

  • Call us on 0300 330 1347

For HC2 and HC3 certificates

  • Call us on 0300 330 1343

Lines are available Monday to Friday from 8am to 6pm and Saturday from 9am to 3pm (Your call will be charged at a local rate).
