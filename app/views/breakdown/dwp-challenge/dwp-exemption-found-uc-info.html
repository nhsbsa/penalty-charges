How to avoid further enquiry letters

We match exemptions using a combination of your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth

Make sure these are correct at your GP surgery, dental practice, and any benefits organisations have your correct details.

Keep your details up to date or you may receive further enquiry letters in the future.

Important information for any future claims

Your Universal Credit exemption must have been valid at the time you claim help with your health costs.

Check that you are still entitled to free prescriptions or dental care before making a claim.

You are entitled to claim free prescriptions if your earnings in the last assessment period were:

  • £435 or less
  • £935 or less if your Universal Credit includes a payment for a child or you have limited capability for work or work-related activity.

If you claim Universal Credit as a couple, the earnings limit applies to the joint income of you and your partner
