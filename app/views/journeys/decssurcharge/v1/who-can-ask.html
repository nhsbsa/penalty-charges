Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

Who can ask for a Penalty Charge Notice review?

We understand that you may have made an honest mistake and didn’t mean to do anything wrong.

If you have an exceptional reason why you claimed a free or reduced cost dental treatment you weren't entitled to, you can ask for a review.

Reasons we won’t usually accept

We won't usually review your Penalty Charge Notice if:

  • your exemption certificate had expired
  • you feel that you were misadvised by your dental practice
  • you thought that your benefit entitled you to claim

What to do next

If you have an exceptional reason why you made the claim at the time, you can contact us to review your Penalty Charge Notice.

If you’re not entitled to a review

You must pay the total amount of your Penalty Charge Notice.
