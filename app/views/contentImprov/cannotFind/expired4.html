We cannot find your maternity exemption certificate

We cannot find your maternity exemption certificate for the dates between 1 December and 31 December 2018.

But you can still apply if you were pregnant when you claimed the free prescription (the date on your Penalty Charge Notice), or you had given birth in the previous 12 months.

What to do next

Pay

You can pay the total amount of £126.40 by debit or credit card, or Direct Debit.








Pay and apply

Pay the original £8.60 prescription charge, and apply for a maternity exemption certificate.







Send proof

Send us proof to show that you were entitled to free prescriptions or had a valid maternity exemption certificate between 1 December and 31 December 2018.




Exceptional circumstances

If you were not entitled to free prescriptions but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice.
