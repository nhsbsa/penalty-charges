What to do next

Send us proof to show that you were entitled to free or reduced cost dental treatment between 1 November and 30 November 2017.

If you do not send us proof, or we cannot find your exemption, you will be sent a penalty charge notice.

If you were not entitled to free or reduced cost dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your penalty charge notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
