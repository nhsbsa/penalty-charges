Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

We need proof that you were pregnant when you claimed free or reduced cost dental treatment, or had given birth in the previous 12 months

You now have 14 days from today to send us your proof.

Type of proof we need to see

  • a MatB1 form
  • a Notification of Birth form (issued by a registered midwife)
  • a Birth or still birth certificate
  • your maternity exemption certificate

How to send your proof

It’s as easy as sending a photo on your phone

  1. 1

    Take a photo of your proof using a mobile phone or digital camera.

    It is important that the details are clearly shown in the photo. You can send a photo of:

    A MatB1 form issued by a GP or registered midwife

    A Notification of Birth form issued by a registered midwife

    A Birth or stillbirth certificate

    A clear scan or photographed image of your exemption certificate card

    A clear screenshot or photographed image of your digital exemption certificate on your mobile device, laptop, or desktop computer

  2. 2

    Check the image is clear and you can read the information.

  3. 3

    Send the photo as an email attachment to: nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

    You must include your Penalty Charge Reference number 01234567890.

    We need to be able to confirm your identity, so your email must also include your:

    • first name and surname (family name)
    • date of birth
    • address including postcode
  4. 4

    Once we receive your email with your proof, we will check it and contact you within 2 working days.

Or you can send your proof by post

If you don’t have access to a digital camera you can photocopy your proof and send the copy to us. You must also include the following information:

  • your Penalty Charge Notice reference number 01234567890
  • first name and surname (family name)
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

Post a copy of your proof to:

Dental Treatment Charges Team
NHSBSA, Bridge House
152 Pilgrim Street
Newcastle Upon Tyne
NE1 6SN

Once we receive your proof by post, we will check it and contact you within 5 working days.
