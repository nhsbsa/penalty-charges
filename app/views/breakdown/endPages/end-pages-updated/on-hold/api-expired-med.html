Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

Your exemption certificate was not valid at the time you claimed

This may have been because your certificate had expired or you didn't get the certificate in time.

You’ve also told us you have a medical condition. Although you didn’t have a valid medical exemption certificate when you claimed a free prescription, you still have the chance to apply.

What to do next

Choose one of the following options:

  • Pay the original £8.60 prescription charge, and apply for a medical exemption certificate. If your application is succesful you won’t have to pay your Penalty Charge.
  • Pay the total amount of £68.60.

If you were not entitled to free prescriptions but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
